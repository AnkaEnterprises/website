/02 — Philosophy

Three rules we don't break.

Every product we ship is filtered through the same three beliefs about what good software looks and feels like. Nothing exotic — just things we won't compromise on.

01 / Simplicity

Remove until it hurts. Then remove a little more.

The best apps do one thing exceptionally well. We strip away everything that isn't essential — settings screens, onboarding flows, dashboards full of numbers nobody reads.

02 / Privacy

Your data stays on your device. Not a marketing slogan.

No accounts. No analytics SDKs. No selling behavior to ad networks. Our apps work fully offline by design — because nothing is more private than data that never leaves the phone.

03 / Velocity

Ship, listen, iterate. Repeat indefinitely.

We'd rather learn from real users than guess in isolation. Every app we make is improved weekly based on what people actually do with it — not what they say in surveys.

/03 — Focus areas

Focused tools for everyday life.

We concentrate on categories where small, well-designed apps are still rare. No social, no AI chatbots, no NFTs — just tools we'd want to use ourselves.
