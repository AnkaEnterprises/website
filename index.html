Our Philosophy

Built on three principles.

Every product we ship is guided by the same core beliefs about what great software looks and feels like.

01

Radical Simplicity

We remove everything that doesn't need to be there. The best apps do one thing exceptionally well, with no unnecessary complexity in the way.

02

Privacy by Default

User data stays on the user's device. No accounts required, no tracking, no selling data — ever.

03

Ship & Iterate

We launch early and improve constantly. Perfect is the enemy of shipped — we'd rather learn from real users than guess in isolation.
